euclidean distance vs. pearson correlation
    euclidean distance is simple pythagorean distance
    pearson correlation measures how well the data fits a straight line
        closer points are to the line -> higher the pearson score; max is 1, min is -1
        corrects grade inflation
        
        
user-based vs item-based filtering
    user-based finds similar users and ranks based on that however users can be dynamic 
    item-based finds similarities between items, less dynamic
